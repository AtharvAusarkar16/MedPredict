About MedPredict

Transforming healthcare through artificial intelligence and data-driven insights

Our Mission

MedPredict aims to revolutionize healthcare planning by providing accurate, AI-powered predictions of hospital stay duration. Our platform helps medical professionals optimize resource allocation, improve patient care planning, and enhance operational efficiency in healthcare facilities.

By leveraging machine learning algorithms trained on extensive medical datasets, we deliver predictions with 15 engineered features and no multicollinearity, enabling hospitals to make informed decisions and provide better patient experiences.

How It Works

Our system analyzes multiple patient factors including age, severity of illness, admission type, financial deposit, and visitor frequency to predict hospital stay duration. The machine learning model was trained on over 5,000 medical records using Random Forest algorithms, ensuring robust and reliable predictions.

The prediction process is instantaneous, providing results in real-time while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security. All processing occurs locally without storing sensitive patient information.
